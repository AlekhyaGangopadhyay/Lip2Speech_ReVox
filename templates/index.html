How it works
01
Face & Lip Region Extraction
OpenCV's Haar Cascade locates the face, then mathematically slices the lower-half mouth area across 25 frames, cropping to 112×112 px.
02
MobileNetV2-BiLSTM Inference
MobileNetV2 extracts spatial features from frames, and a 2-layer BiLSTM processes them over time to decode the sequence of words.
03
T5 LLM Correction & TTS
Argmax predictions are cleaned and refined with a local HuggingFace T5-base model, then spoken aloud offline using pyttsx3.
Lip2Speech Built with PyTorch · Flask · pyttsx3 · OpenCV · HuggingFace T5
